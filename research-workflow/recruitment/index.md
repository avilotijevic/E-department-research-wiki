# Recruiting participants

This section describes how to recruit participants for studies conducted
at the E-department.

Participant recruitment usually goes either through the SONA system (where the participants are first-year psychology students) or the Paid Participants Pool (PPP).

- [SONA system](sona-system.md)
- [Paid Participants Pool (PPP)](PPP-system.md)
