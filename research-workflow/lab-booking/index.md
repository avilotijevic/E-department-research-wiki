# Booking a lab

This section provides information on how to book experimental laboratories and which facilities are available in the department.  
Before starting data collection, researchers must ensure that the appropriate lab is reserved and that the required equipment is available.

---

## Overview

Experimental Psychology provides several laboratories equipped for different types of experiments, including:

- Eye-tracking studies
- EEG recordings
- Neurostimulation experiments
- Behavioral testing

Different labs contain different equipment and may require specific preparation before use.

## Good practice when booking labs

When scheduling lab time, please consider the following:

- Book laboratories **only after your experiment has been properly tested, ethical approval has been obtained, and the study has been approved on SONA**.
- Sharing is caring: avoid reserving an entire week unless no one else needs the lab for testing.
- Cancel reservations if you no longer need them.
- Coordinate with other researchers when running longer studies.

Proper scheduling ensures that laboratories remain accessible to everyone in the department.
