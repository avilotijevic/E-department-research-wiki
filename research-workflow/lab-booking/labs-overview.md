The Experimental Psychology department uses several laboratories equipped with specialized systems for behavioral and psychophysiological experiments. Each lab supports different types of experiments depending on the available equipment.

Before planning a study, make sure that the selected lab supports the methods and equipment required for your experiment.

## Important notes
